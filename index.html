Welcome to Jacob Levi Limited, home of the Hold The Mic brand! I’m Azan White, the founder, designer, developer, and everything in between.

As a one-person team, I created Hold The Mic to bring my passion for gaming to life through unique, engaging, and fun mobile games, available on the App Store.

Since launching Jacob Levi Limited in 2014, I’ve been dedicated to crafting games that are not only entertaining but also push the boundaries of creativity and innovation. Every game under the Hold The Mic brand is a product of my vision, hard work, and commitment to delivering an unforgettable experience to players around the world.

Being a solo developer means I’m involved in every step of the process—from brainstorming and coding to design and testing. This hands-on approach allows me to maintain a personal touch and ensure the quality of every game I release.

At Hold The Mic, I believe in creating games that reflect my core values: creativity, immersive player engagement, and continuous innovation. Every game is designed to spark imagination, provide players with interactive and memorable experiences, and push the limits of what’s possible in mobile gaming.

I’m passionate about crafting games that are not only fun but also leave a lasting impression, making Hold The Mic a brand that players can trust for high-quality, engaging entertainment.

Thank you for supporting Hold The Mic and downloading my games! Your feedback and enjoyment fuel my passion, and I can’t wait to show you what’s coming next.
